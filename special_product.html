About 6" FLour Mill Stone

This stone is used in a flour mill, typically measuring 6 inches in diameter. These stones are crucial components in traditional flour mills and are responsible for grinding grains into flour. These stones are used for grinding grains, such as wheat, corn, or rice, into flour. They work by rotating against each other, with one stone being stationary (the bed stone) and the other (the runner stone) moving on top of it.
